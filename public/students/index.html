The designation

Demonstrated. Not attended.

KARST Solutions Engineer is earned against a published four-part standard. The evidence is work in real use.

  1. 01 · PRACTICE
    Run your own practice.

    A documented AI workflow you use for real work — yours, not a worksheet.

  2. 02 · BUILD
    Ship real tools.

    Three or more tools in use at your campus or the district office.

  3. 03 · TRANSFER
    Teach the method.

    One session where other students learn how you build.

  4. 04 · PRESENT
    Demo day.

    A capstone presented to district leadership.
